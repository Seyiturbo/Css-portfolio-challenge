About Me

My name is Seyi Tubosun. I am a lawyer, a pianist, and an award-winning internationally published photographer. I am also the CEO & Creative Director of Seyiturbo Studios INC. I have an outstanding experience of over 12 years in Photography and a robust portfolio that cuts across wedding, portrait, and studio photography. I was privileged to cover the YouWIn Awards and Sure P Awards at the Presidential Villa's Banquet hall in 2014 among other high-profile jobs. I was a Lecturer at Pixels Photography Academy in the Year 2010 training over 15 students at the institute. I have a team of seasoned professional photographers that we work together as a team in Africa, Europe, and the United States. Currently, I am domiciled in the United kingdom and a student at Birmingham University Bootcamp programme studying Front-end Web development and UX.
